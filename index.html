About Me

Chemistry at the frontier of computation & analysis.

I am Protyoi Chakraborty, a chemist with a BSc from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST), Sylhet, Bangladesh. My work sits at the intersection of computational drug discovery and rigorous analytical science.

Currently, I serve as an Associate Scientist in Analytical R&D at Square Pharmaceuticals PLC, where I lead analytical method validation of finished products including Sitafloxacin, Roxadustat, Voriconazole Injection, and Naproxen Sodium Tablets, aligning with ICH Q2(R2) and global regulatory requirements (FDA, WHO, TGA, NPRA).

In parallel, I am a Remote Research Assistant at the Computer in Chemistry & Medicine Lab, Dhaka, studying the physicochemical, spectral, and toxicological profiles of NSAIDs and their degradants using DFT, molecular docking, and MD simulation. A published letter in The Lancet and an ongoing PLOS ONE submission mark the beginning of my scientific publishing journey.

Research

Areas of Investigation

My research interests span computational pharmacology, analytical chemistry, and environmental toxicology — united by the goal of safer, more effective therapeutics.

💊

Computer-Aided Drug Design

Applying molecular docking (COX-2, PDB: 5F19), DFT thermodynamics, and 100-ns MD simulations to evaluate the binding affinities and stabilities of drug degradants, with ADMET and PASS profiling.

Primary Focus
🧪

Analytical & Computational Chemistry

Computational UV–Vis spectral analysis, frontier molecular orbital characterization, and quantum-chemical modeling of NSAIDs and their degradants to understand electronic transitions and reactivity.

Active
🌿

Toxicology & Environmental Chemistry

ADMET profiling for absorption, BBB permeability, and acute oral toxicity. Environmental persistence analysis of degradants and implications for safer synthesis and green pharmacy.

Active
🏥

Neuropharmacology & Cancer Therapeutics

Investigating analgesic, antipyretic, and anti-inflammatory potentials via PASS predictions, alongside risks of nephro-, hepato-, and hematotoxicity in drug degradant molecules.

Interest
⚗️

Pharmacokinetics & ADMET

Predicting drug-likeness, oral bioavailability, and metabolic stability for novel candidate molecules using in silico tools including Swiss ADME and pkCSM platforms.

Interest
🔬

Bioelectronic Medical Devices

Academic collaboration on conductive hydrogel-based bioelectronic devices with PI Dr. Md Rezaul Karim (Yeungnam University, South Korea) — manuscript under preparation.
